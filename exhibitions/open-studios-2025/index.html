Open Studios 2025

A weekend with every studio open to the public across all four programs.

About the exhibition

Open Studios invites the public into the school for a weekend, with every studio open across the four programs.

Expect work-in-progress, conversations with artists, and pop-up print sales.

Details

Dates
Nov 14 – Nov 16, 2025
Venue
All buildings, 1156 Chapel Street
Reception
Studios open 12–6pm daily
Department
School-wide

Free and open to the public. Plan your visit.
