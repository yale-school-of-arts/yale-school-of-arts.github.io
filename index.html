Featured

A studio of your own, and a community to push against

Every student gets the space, tools and time to build a serious body of work — alongside weekly critique with faculty who are practicing artists and designers. It’s small by design: roughly 55 admitted each year, a 4:1 student–faculty ratio, and four studio disciplines under one roof.

Our method

Make. Look. Revise. Repeat.

An art education isn’t a style you’re handed — it’s judgment you build, in the studio, in critique, in public. That’s what two years here are for.
