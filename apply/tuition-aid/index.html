Apply · Tuition, Fees & Aid

What it costs — and how aid works

Annual tuition is $48,200. Cost shouldn't decide who gets to make work, so every admitted student is considered for need-based aid.

Financial aid

Ways the school helps

Financial aid options
SourceHow it works
Need-based grantsAwarded to admitted students based on demonstrated financial need. No separate application.
Fellowships & teachingSelected students receive named fellowships or paid teaching/shop roles.
External fundingWe help you identify outside grants, loans and scholarships.
Application fee waiversAvailable for applicants for whom the $100 fee is a barrier.

There is no separate scholarship application: when you apply for admission, you are automatically considered for need-based aid. Aid offers accompany admission decisions so you can make an informed choice.
